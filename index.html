A Chance to Share Our Passion

ECE Outreach is the Electrical and Computer engineering (ECE) department outreach program at Colorado State University.
Having a vertically integrated team with members of all experience levels we are a diverse group of students who want to share what we love about ECE.
Our group focuses around both the development of demos and presenting to others.
We communicate with high schools around Colorado to visit and share what we've developed.
We discuss and answer questions about ECE for anyone interested and present our demos while providing new learning opportunities.

A Chance to Inspire

As an outreach program our goal for ECE Outreach is to reach those outside of the Electrical and Computer engineering (ECE) department.
We actively work to attend and create events where we can share our experiences in ECE and encourage others to joins us in our field.
Outside of presenting our secondary goal is to create demos that assist us with explaining and showing the concepts found in ECE to others.
We strive to create an environment where learning about ECE is fun and easy and everyone is encouraged to take part.
